**WHY CUTBOOK?**

CutBook was built specifically for small to medium-sized salon businesses in Bangladesh. We understand the challenges of managing employees, tracking daily work, calculating commissions, and maintaining accurate records. That's why we created CutBook - a simple, powerful tool that helps you:

• Save time on daily record-keeping
• Eliminate calculation errors
• Monitor business performance
• Motivate your team with transparency
• Make informed business decisions

4. Start tracking work entries
