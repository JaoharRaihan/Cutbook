# CutBook - Salon & Barber Shop Management

**Manage your salon or barber shop with ease. Track services, employees, income, and generate reports - all offline!**

---

## 📱 Features

###  Service Management
- Create and organize services by category (Haircut, Shave, Facial, Massage, etc.)
- Set default prices for each service
- Quick service selection during entry

### 👥 Employee Management
- Add unlimited employees
- Track individual performance
- Set custom commission rates
- View employee statistics

### 💰 Work Entry Tracking
- Quick entry creation
- Multiple payment methods (Cash, bKash, Card, Nagad)
- Add tips and notes
- Edit history for accountability

### 📊 Reports & Analytics
- Daily income summaries
- Payment method breakdown
- Employee performance rankings
- Date range reports
- Export capabilities

### 🌐 Bengali Language Support
- Full Bengali interface (বাংলা)
- Bengali numerals (০-৯)
- BDT currency formatting (৳)
- Toggle between English and Bengali

### 📴 Offline First
- Works without internet
- Data stored locally
- No subscription required
- Privacy-friendly
