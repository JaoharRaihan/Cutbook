# Privacy Policy for CutBook

**Effective Date**: February 14, 2026
**Last Updated**: February 14, 2026

## Introduction

CutBook ("we", "our", "us", or "the app") is a salon and barbershop management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using CutBook, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address** (for authentication)
- **Password** (encrypted and stored securely)
- **Name** (display name for your account)

### 2. Business Information

When you use the app, we collect:

- **Organization name** (your salon/shop name)
- **Employee information** (names, phone numbers, email addresses, commission rates)
- **Service information** (service names, prices, categories)
- **Work entry records** (employee, service, price, tip, payment method, date/time)

### 3. Automatically Collected Information

- **Device information** (device model, operating system version)
- **App usage data** (features used, crash reports)
- **Authentication tokens** (to keep you logged in)

### 4. Information We Do NOT Collect

- Credit card or payment information (not processed by us)
- Location data
- Photos or media
- Contacts
- SMS or call logs
- Third-party account information (beyond authentication)

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Store and sync your business data
   - Calculate commissions and generate reports
   - Enable multi-device access

2. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Debug and fix technical issues
   - Monitor app performance

3. **Communication**
   - Send important account notifications
   - Respond to support requests
   - Provide updates about the app

4. **Security**
   - Prevent unauthorized access
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Maintain data integrity

---

## How We Store Your Information

### Cloud Storage

- All data is stored securely using **Google Firebase** (Google Cloud Platform)
- Firebase is a trusted, industry-standard cloud service
- Data is encrypted both **in transit** (HTTPS/TLS) and **at rest** (AES-256)

### Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- **Inactive accounts**: Data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity
- **Deleted accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

### Offline Access

- Recent data is cached locally on your device for offline access
- Local cache is automatically cleared when you log out
