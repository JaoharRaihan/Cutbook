# 🔒 Privacy Policy for CutBook

**Last Updated:** December 13, 2025

## Introduction

CutBook ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**CutBook does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

### What Data is Stored Locally:
- Organization information (name, settings)
- Employee details (names, phone numbers, commission rates)
- Service information (names, prices, categories)
- Work entry records (services performed, payments)
- App preferences (language selection)

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to any server.

## Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:
- App functionality (displaying dashboards, generating reports)
- Your convenience (persisting settings across app launches)

We do NOT:
- Send data to external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Track user behavior
- Display advertisements
- Require user accounts or registration

## Data Security

- All data stays on your device
- No internet connection required for app functionality
- You have full control to delete data at any time
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

## Third-Party Services

CutBook does NOT use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Children's Privacy

CutBook does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for business owners and employees.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:
1. Going to App Settings
2. Selecting "Clear All Data"
3. Or uninstalling the app

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app.
